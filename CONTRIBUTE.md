# The Promura Project #

![promura-project](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/110672536/183131595-afeb1dec-1c84-436c-9a50-90468f9ec3ec.png)

The objective of **_The Promura Project_** is to collate as much information as possible about two very obscure and rare camera lenses: the **_Auto-Promura 28mm C.P. Hi-Lux MC_** and the **_Auto-Promura 20mm C.P. Hi-Lux MC_**. **{[1](https://github.com/martbetz/thepromuraproject#readme)}**

---

### Contribution Guidelines ###

Needless to say, any additonal information/corrections are very welcome! In particular, anything you can contribute to the following would be very much appreciated:

- further information about **_Nissin Koki Co. Ltd._**

- full-frame revews of the **_Auto-Promura 28mm C.P. Hi-Lux MC_**

- full-frame and crop-sensor revews of the **_Auto-Promura 20mm C.P. Hi-Lux MC_**

Please provide references for all submissions — i.e.  hyperlinks to reliable online sources, and photos for owned lenses that clearly show the serial number and mount type.
